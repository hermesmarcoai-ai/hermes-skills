## Soglie Sentiment
| Fear & Greed | Azione |
|--------------|--------|
| < 25 | Buy zone (contrarian) |
| 25-45 | Accumulation |
| 45-55 | Wait |
| 55-75 | Take profits |
| > 75 | Sell/exit |

## BUY Checklist (tutti i 4 allineati = high conviction)
1. Fear & Greed < 30
2. Social volume rising + sentiment positivo
3. Technical: RSI < 35, MACD bullish crossover, at support
4. Catalizzatore: listing imminente, news positive

## SELL Checklist (2+ presenti = caution)
1. Fear & Greed > 70
2. Social mentions in picco, copertura mainstream
3. Technical: RSI > 65, MACD bearish divergence
4. On-chain: Whale distribution
